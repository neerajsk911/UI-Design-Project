About Me

Finally you made it to my blog!!! Not a professional blog writer by the way, but I do feel blogs are the strongest way to express one's talents. Blogs express the hidden talents of people who are either not too confident to present it directly to others and hence keep it confined within themselves. Here you Go!! Its time to explore something about ourselves!!!

Talking about myself, I'm a native of Kerala which is indeed my motherland as well.The green pleasant atmosphere and the cool climate due to its edges beings surrounded by coasts makes it heaven! Well I'm not a geographic analyser indeed to explain all these but it only means how the climate here feels so amusing. There is no wonder why its called the God's Own Country!!!

My Childhood in Kerala was not too long as for we resided in Delhi. I was a student at Kerala Education Society Secondary School. It was my place of adobe of knowledge. The memories in that campus also do have a large place in my heart!

I did travel back here for my vacations and always feel a sense of Gloominess after spending sometime here. The love and care that we get from my Grandmother and our relatives is not acquaintant in our Cities. The time we spend playing and making us feel in heaven is the time when we interact and do any random stuff with our cousins and call it a 'Game'!. I must say my Childhood has been awesome only because of all these. Without all these my Childhood would not have must to educe upon.

Well now look Where We Are! Time has passed inconspicously and now we have passed that point where everything was a "Mere Game" to What is called a "Reality"! Now being a Computer Science Engineer life seems so like "Micro-Chip" coded with pleasure at the beginning stages which can be even termed as the "Lowest Level in the Game of Life!" then heading slightly to difficult stages until we feel ourselves relaxed after getting settled with a Job and Family. I have actively did researches on Many fields of Networking Security as well as Coding practices to make me better every growing day. Well life the technology keeps on growing as a result requiring more and more of Immense work on these. Well will discuss more of these as we go!! So lets fire this journey Up!
